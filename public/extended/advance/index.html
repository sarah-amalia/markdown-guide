Extended Syntax in Markdown

Horizontal Rules

To create horizontal rule on your page, add 3 or more hyphens --- - - -, arterisks *** * * *, or underscores ___ _ _ _ in a single line. These will produce horizontal line.

Markdown syntax:

---
- - -
***
* * *
___
_ _ _

Tables

To produce tables in your page I will separate into 2 format.

  1. Create rows header
    Use pipes | to separate each rows. The example will be:
| Title | Descriptions |
  1. Create each column’s header
    Add three or more hyphens--- within pipes | below the header rows.
|---|---|
  1. Followed the rest of content inside pipes | on the table.
| Topic | Paragraph |

The format would be:

| Title | Descriptions |
|---|---|
| Topic | Paragraph |

The rendered output:

Title Descriptions
Topic Paragraph

Alerts/Admonitions/Callouts

To call attention of critical information, Majority of Markdown applications don’t support this syntax. You can use this syntax of tools that support GitHub-Flavored Markdown (GFM) Alerts. There are 5 types of alerts available and commonly used. To create alerts on markdown in GFM supported tools add blockquotes syntax by startting with greater-than symbol > followed by the alert type in a set of square brackets [].

The format is:

> [!ALERT TYPE]
> Your alert content.

The common types of alert available including:

NOTE: Callout information audiences should know.

> [!NOTE]
> This is note.

TIP: Callout helful advice for audiences.

> [!TIP]
> This is tip.

IMPORTANT: Crucial information for audiences.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This is important key information.

WARNING: Callout problems should avoid by the audiences.

> [!WARNING]
> This is warning.

CAUTION: Potential negative outcomes or risks about actions audiences should know.

> [!CAUTION]
> This is caution.
